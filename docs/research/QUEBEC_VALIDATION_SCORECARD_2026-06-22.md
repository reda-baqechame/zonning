# Quebec ZONNING Validation Scorecard

Date: 2026-06-22
Purpose: decide whether ZONNING has a real Quebec wedge before building

## Hypothesis To Prove

Quebec specialty contractors and suppliers will pay for qualified opportunity intelligence if it saves chase time, reveals useful early signals, and explains Quebec-specific source/compliance/site risk better than their current workflow.

## Segment Scorecard

Score each segment from 1 to 5.

| Segment | Pain | Budget | Access | Data fit | Competitor weakness | Speed to payment | Total |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Specialty subcontractors | 5 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 4 | 4 | 25 |
| Material/equipment suppliers | 5 | 4 | 4 | 5 | 4 | 4 | 26 |
| Mid-size estimator/BD teams | 5 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 22 |
| Developers/site scouts | 4 | 5 | 2 | 5 | 3 | 2 | 21 |
| Public-contract bidders | 4 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 20 |
| Large GCs | 4 | 5 | 2 | 3 | 2 | 1 | 17 |

Recommendation:

- First validation segment: material/equipment suppliers and specialty subcontractors.
- Secondary validation segment: mid-size estimator/BD teams.
- Do not lead with large GCs or developers.

## Must-Hit Numbers

The wedge is worth product work if:

- 5 users receive concierge briefs.
- 3 ask for a second weekly brief.
- 2 take a real business action.
- 3 say at least one item was not quickly findable in their normal workflow.
- 2 accept a paid pilot.
- Average user reaction score across top 10 items is 3.0 or higher.

The wedge fails if:

- Average reaction score is below 2.0.
- No one takes action.
- No one accepts a pilot price.
- Users say the value is just cheaper tender listings.
- Users distrust permit-derived leads after seeing real examples.

## Interview Outcome Matrix

| Signal | Meaning | Decision |
|---|---|---|
| "I already saw all of these in MERX/SEAO" | No differentiation | Improve source mix or pivot |
| "This saves me from checking 5 places" | Workflow pain confirmed | Continue |
| "I would call this owner/GC" | Actionability confirmed | Continue |
| "This is too early to use" | Pre-RFP signal weak | Narrow to tenders/permits |
| "This permit is useful but I need owner/contact/trade details" | Permit wedge needs enrichment | Build enrichment manually first |
| "Compliance notes are helpful" | Quebec-specific fit matters | Increase RBQ/AMP/RENA layer |
| "I only need official docs/submission" | MERX/SEAO-owned need | Do not build |
| "Send me next week" | Retention signal | Ask for payment |
