## Decision This Package Supports

Do not build more features until the team validates whether Quebec contractors and suppliers will pay for:

> Quebec construction opportunity intelligence before and around the official tender, using permits, SEAO, RBQ/AMP/RENA fit, municipal signals, and site/property context.

## Working Positioning

ZONNING should not try to be a bigger MERX or SEAO. It should be a smaller, sharper Quebec intelligence layer that helps teams decide what to chase, why it fits, and what to do next.

## Stronger V2 Recommendation

Start with Quebec specialty contractors and construction suppliers. Validate a weekly Quebec Qualified Opportunity Brief before building more product. The brief should combine permits, SEAO/public opportunities, RBQ/AMP/RENA fit, municipal signals, source freshness, and site context into chase/watch/ignore decisions.

## Must-Prove Before Building

- At least 8 of 15 focused customers describe the problem as painful and current tools as incomplete.
- At least 5 of 15 agree to test with real company criteria.
- At least 3 of 15 show payment intent or accept a pilot price.
- At least 5 users say ZONNING found or qualified something they would not have found quickly in SEAO/MERX.

## Must-Delete Claims Until Proven

- Complete Quebec procurement coverage.
- Replacement for SEAO or MERX.
- Never miss an opportunity.
- All municipalities indexed.
- Comprehensive compliance verification.
- AI claims without source-level proof.
